Welcome to FriendList!

FriendList is a media portal dedicated to promoting gaming culture and Valorant esport competitions among students.

It provides a variety of information about Valorant esport at the ITBSS campus, focusing on reporting various events, tournament updates, team profiles, and more.

FRIENDLIST Vision

To be the primary source of information and promotion for Valorant esport activities among students at the ITBSS campus.

FRIENDLIST Mission

  • Providing up-to-date information about Valorant esport tournaments, events, and news within the campus environment.
  • Supporting the coaching and development of student Valorant teams through team profiles, match analysis, and other means.
  • Offering information on joining the Valorant esport community at the ITBSS campus.
